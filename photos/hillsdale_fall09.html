Hillsdale, Michigan, fall of 2009

The IOA's fifth and final race of the season was in Hillsdale, Michigan on September 11, 12, and 13. We had some pretty good racing and brought back some great photos. We were lucky enough to get this beautiful course twice this year. It was an excellent way to end an excellent season!
